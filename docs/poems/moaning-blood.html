Moaning Blood

Plans my father made

    lie before me

    wrapped in fear

    and covered by dust.


I have closets and alleyways

    packed in the crevices of my heart;

    often moaning with blood,

    I live in the stench of fear.


I do not recognize sins

    I have grown into

    until I taste bad blood

    from my swollen tongue.


I hope you may ease into life

    and rest gently

    at its end.
